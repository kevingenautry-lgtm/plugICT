Our Promise

We want you to be confident in your purchase. If the product has a genuine defect — the vault fails to load, search returns no results, or the setup scripts error out — and we cannot resolve it within 7 days of your purchase, you are entitled to a full refund.

What Qualifies

  • Vault file fails to decrypt with the issued license key.
  • Setup scripts error out on a supported platform (Windows / macOS / Linux) and we cannot resolve the issue.
  • MCP server fails to start after following the setup instructions.

What Doesn't Qualify

  • Buyer's remorse or change of mind after downloading the vault.
  • Compatibility issues with unsupported software or custom configurations.
  • Issues caused by modifying vault files or setup scripts.
