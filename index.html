Questions, answered.

Do I need to be technical to set this up? +

No. One setup file does everything, then you paste a small config into your AI agent. A built-in --doctor command tells you exactly what to fix, in plain English.

Does it work without internet? +

Searching never calls a cloud API. Your first search downloads two small models once; after that the vault is fully offline. (Your AI agent, like Claude Desktop, uses its own internet connection as normal.)

Can I use it with ChatGPT or Claude on the web? +

Today it connects to local MCP agents — Claude Desktop, Cursor, Hermes. A hosted connector for web AIs is on the roadmap; lifetime buyers get first access.

What exactly do I download? +

An encrypted knowledge vault plus a lightweight app. Your personal license.key (emailed to you) unlocks it. The license is tied to you and traceable, so please don't share it.

How can I pay? +

DuitNow QR and FPX online banking for Malaysia, international cards via Stripe, or USDT (Solana / TRC20) for crypto. However you pay, your license is emailed to you and the product works exactly the same.

What are the system requirements? +

Windows, macOS or Linux with Python 3.9+. The setup script handles the rest in an isolated environment, so it won't interfere with anything else on your computer.
