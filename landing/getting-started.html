ICT Vault

You're in. Let's get it running.

Three short steps: download, unlock with your license, connect your AI. Takes about three minutes.

1

Download & unzip

Grab the vault package and unzip it anywhere (e.g. your Desktop):

⬇️ Download ICT Vault (encrypted)

Then drop the license.key from your purchase email into that same folder, next to setup.bat.

The vault is encrypted — it does nothing without your license.key. Keep that file private; it's tied to you.
2

Run setup

Double-click setup.bat. It builds an isolated environment and checks everything. Then search with:

vault.bat "what is a fair value gap?"

In Terminal, from the unzipped folder:

./setup.sh
./vault.sh "what is a fair value gap?"
Something not working? Run vault.bat --doctor (or ./vault.sh --doctor). It tells you exactly what to fix.
3

Connect your AI agent

Open your Claude Desktop config and add the entry from examples/claude_desktop_config.json. Restart Claude — it now has a search_ict tool.

Add examples/cursor_mcp.json to your Cursor MCP settings, then ask about any ICT concept without leaving your editor.

Point your Hermes profile at examples/hermes_config.yaml. Your agent auto-discovers the vault's tools.

Full walkthrough with screenshots: see docs/AI-AGENT-GUIDE.md in your download.

That's it 🎉

Ask your AI things like "How does ICT teach the Silver Bullet setup?" — it'll answer with real clips and timestamps.
